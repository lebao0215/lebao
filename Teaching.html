About Research Teaching CV



Courses Taught:
  • STAT/IST 557, Data Mining, A PhD-level course introducing data mining, statistical/machine learning, and their applications in large complex data sets.

  • STAT554, Categorical Data Analysis, A PhD-level course discussing how to formulate, fit, select, and assess a wide variety of models for discrete data.

  • STAT897D, Applied Data Mining, A master-level course providing techniques and software to automate big data analysis.

  • STAT/MATH415, Introduction to Mathematical Statistics, An undergraduate-level course introducing statistical inference, e.g., estimation, hypothesis testing, regression, non-parametric statistics, and Bayesian statistics.

  • STAT497, Introduction to Statistical Research, An undergraduate-level course providing new researchers the necessary skills to engage in independent research projects, critically evaluate research outcomes, and effectively communicate research findings.
